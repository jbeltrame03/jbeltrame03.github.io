B-52 Replacement: An Upgrade and Modernization





Background:

In my freetime, I wanted to work on designing an aircraft. I decided that I would create an aircraft to replace the aging B-52 Stratofortress. My new design would incorporate modern technology, aerodynamics and modern concepts to try and find something that can far exceed that of the B-52.



Design Requirements:

The requirements were based off the B-52 specifications. I took what the B-52 is capable of in terms of performance and payload, and increased these metrics for its replacement.

  • Payload: < 100,000 lb
  • Range: 13,000 nmi
  • Cruise Speed: < Mach 0.85
  • Ceiling Altitude: 60,000 ft
  • Same footprint as the B-52 (see below)
  • Carry an armament consisting of GBU-57s (MOP) as well as GBU-43s (MOAB)
  • All armament stored internally
  • After an ingress cruise of 6,500 nmi, can loiter for 2 hours, then cruise out for 6,500 nmi without refueling.
